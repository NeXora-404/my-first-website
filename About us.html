About Us: Mr. Telapoka Secondary School

Welcome to Mr. Telapoka Secondary School, a vibrant institution dedicated to nurturing young minds and building the leaders of tomorrow.
Nestled in a serene, picturesque countryside setting, our school combines the beauty of nature with a robust academic foundation to provide an ideal environment for holistic growth.

Our Vision

To empower students with high-quality education, strong moral values, and the critical thinking skills needed to thrive in an ever-changing world, while keeping them deeply rooted in our rich cultural heritage.

Our Campus & Facilities

Our campus beautifully blends modern educational infrastructure with traditional aesthetics to create a unique learning atmosphere:
  • Modern Academic Blocks: Well-ventilated, two-story buildings equipped with spacious classrooms and open corridors that foster a collaborative learning environment.
  • The Heritage Wing:A classic, tin-roofed structure that stands as a proud reminder of our roots and traditional architectural history.
  • The Green Courtyard: A vast, open grass field where students engage in sports, physical activities, and community gatherings under the shade of mature, lush trees.

Why Choose Us?

Holistic Development: We believe in balancing academic excellence with co-curricular activities, encouraging students to explore sports, arts, and leadership.
  • A Safe & Caring Environment: Enclosed within a secure campus perimeter, our school ensures a safe, disciplined, and supportive space for every child.
  • A Community Feeling:From students riding bicycles along our brick-paved paths to the daily assembly under the national flag, we foster a deep sense of patriotism, unity, and belonging.

At Mr. Telapoka Secondary School, we don't just teach lessons—we inspire lifelong learning.
